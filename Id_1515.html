1. Normal Operation (The "Standard" Rule)

In a Delta (Î”\Delta) connection, the windings are connected in parallel with the lines.

  • Voltage Rule: Line Voltage (VLV_L) is exactly equal to Phase Voltage (VPV_P).
  • Current Rule: Line Current (ILI_L) is 3\sqrt{3} times the Phase Current (IPI_P).

Exam Tip: If the question asks "What is the line voltage of a 240V Delta secondary?", the answer is 240V.
