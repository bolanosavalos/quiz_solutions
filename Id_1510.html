3. Recommendation: FMC vs. LFMC

For a motor connection, the choice depends on the environment:

  • FMC (Flexible Metal Conduit): Best for dry, indoor locations. It provides excellent mechanical protection but is not water-tight.
  • LFMC (Liquid-Tight Flexible Metal Conduit): Highly Recommended for most motor installations. Motors often exist in areas requiring wash-downs, or where oil and coolants are present. LFMC has a waterproof jacket that protects the conductors from moisture and vibrations.

  • Practical Minimum: Trade Size 16 (1/2") is the standard for most industrial motor drops to allow for easier pulling and future-proofing.

Recommendation: Use Trade Size 16 (1/2") LFMC. It offers better environmental protection and is easier to work with than the maximum-capacity 3/8" alternative.
