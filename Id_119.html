The correct answer is:

✅ D. Use digital VOM to check from shell to centre contact.

Explanation:

When testing a High Pressure Sodium (HPS) lamp socket for power:

  • You want to verify voltage at the socket where the lamp connects.
  • The shell (outer metal part) of the socket is typically neutral, and the centre contact is live (hot).
  • Therefore, you measure between the shell and the centre contact.

Use a digital volt-ohm meter (VOM) rather than an analog one because:

  • HPS circuits often have electronic ballasts or high-voltage ignitors, and an analog meter could be damaged or load the circuit.
  • A digital VOM provides a safer, more accurate reading.

✅ Answer: D. Use digital VOM to check from shell to centre contact.
