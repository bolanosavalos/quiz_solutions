3-Phase 575V Motor Feeder Calculation

To determine the conductor size for a motor bank, we follow the Canadian Electrical Code (CEC) Rule 28-108. The feeder must have an ampacity of at least 125% of the full-load current (FLC) of the largest motor, plus the sum of the FLCs of all other motors.

1. Full-Load Current (FLC) Data

Using CEC Table 44 for 3-phase, 575V Squirrel Cage Induction Motors (SCIM):

  • 50 HP (Largest): 52 A
  • 40 HP: 41 A
  • 25 HP: 27 A

2. Feeder Ampacity Calculation

  • Largest Motor (50 HP): 52 A x 1.25 = 65 A
  • Sum of remaining motors: 41 A + 27 A = 68 A
  • Total Minimum Feeder Ampacity: 65 A + 68 A = 133 A

3. Conductor Selection (Aluminum R90 / XLPE)

Per Rule 4-006, we must use the 75°C column of Table 4 because standard equipment terminations are typically rated for 75°C, even if the wire (R90) is rated for 90°C.

  • #1/0 AWG Aluminum (75°C): 120 A (Too small)
  • #2/0 AWG Aluminum (75°C): 135 A (Sufficient for 133 A load)
