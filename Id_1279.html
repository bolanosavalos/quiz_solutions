CEC 2024 Requirements

The code specifies that the point of attachment for the service conductors on a service mast must be:

  • Minimum: 150 mm below the service head.
  • Maximum: 300 mm below the service head.

This range ensures that there is enough conductor length to form a proper drip loop (to prevent water from entering the service head) while keeping the conductors close enough to the mast to maintain structural integrity and required clearances.
