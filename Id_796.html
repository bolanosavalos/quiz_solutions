📘 Question

What is the minimum size grounding conductor connected to the frame of an electrolytic water heater?

Choices:
10
6
8
12

✅ How to Solve It (CEC 2024 Method)

1️⃣ An electrolytic water heater is equipment with exposed metal parts.
2️⃣ The frame must be bonded.
3️⃣ Bonding conductors are sized using:

  • Section 10 - Grounding and Bonding
  • Rule 10-614 / 10-616
  • Table 16

There is no special fixed 6 AWG rule in CEC 2024 for electrolytic water heaters.

📊 What Table 16 Tells You

If no breaker size is given, exams expect the minimum copper bonding conductor permitted by Table 16.

For common branch circuits (like a 30A water heater):

  • 30A → #12 AWG copper bonding conductor

And #12 copper is the smallest bonding conductor permitted by the CEC for equipment bonding in typical installations.

🎯 Final Exam Answer

✅ 12 AWG
