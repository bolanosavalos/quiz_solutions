Final Conclusion

The minimum size of copper conductor for a 6V emergency lighting system with 4 amps of current and a 15-meter length is No. 8 AWG.

While a 4A load only requires No. 14 AWG for ampacity, the extreme sensitivity of a 6V system to voltage drop requires the significantly larger No. 8 AWG to ensure the emergency lights receive at least 5.7V at the end of the run.
