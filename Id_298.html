The conducted VA (VAcondVA_{\text{cond}}) for a step-down autotransformer can be calculated using the ratio of the secondary voltage (VSV_S) to the primary voltage (VPV_P) multiplied by the total secondary load (VAtotalVA_{\text{total}}).

The formula is: VAcond=VAtotal×(VSVP)VA_{\text{cond}} = VA_{\text{total}} \times \left(\frac{V_S}{V_P}\right)
