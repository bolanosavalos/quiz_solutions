The correct distance between the seal and the lighting switch (an explosion-proof enclosure containing a device that may produce arcs or sparks) in a Class I, Division 1 location, as per the Canadian Electrical Code (CEC), is 450 mm or less.

The relevant rule in the CEC is typically found in Section 18 (Hazardous Locations) or the former Annex J18

CEC Rule Reference

The requirement is specified in the CEC for conduit seals entering an enclosure that is required to be explosion-proof and contains devices that may produce arcs, sparks, or high temperatures.

  • The seal shall be located as close as practicable to the enclosure, and in no case more than 450 mm450\text{ mm} (1818 in.) from it.
