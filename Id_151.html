Explanation

This is a problem that requires using Ohm's Law and the power formula for electrical circuits. The key assumption is that the resistance (R\boldsymbol{R}) of the heater remains constant despite the change in voltage.

The rated power (PratedP_{\text{rated}}) and rated voltage (VratedV_{\text{rated}}) are used to find the resistance. The power formula is: P=V2RP = \frac{V^2}{R}
