Refer to CEC 2024. Determine the minimum size of electrical non-metallic tubing (ENT) required for the following conductors installed together in one conduit:

  1. 4 ×\times No. 14 AWG TWU
  2. 2 ×\times No. 6 AWG RW90 XLPE 600 V, unjacketed
  3. 8 ×\times No. 10 AWG TWN75 75 °C
  4. 2 ×\times No. 2 AWG R90 XLPE 600 V, unjacketed

Step 1 -- Applicable Rules and Tables

  • Rule 12-910(4): Maximum fill for more than two conductors = 40%
  • Table 6 series: Conductor cross-sectional areas (mm2^2)
  • Table 9G (ENT): Internal cross-sectional area for electrical non-metallic tubing
