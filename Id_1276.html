According to Rule 12-518 of the Canadian Electrical Code (CEC), cable used in exposed wiring must be adequately protected against mechanical damage in specific situations. In your example:

  1. The Height Requirement: Rule 12-518 (1)(b) states that protection is required where the cable is less than 1.5 m above a floor.
  2. The Math: Since your cable runs down and ends at 1 m off the floor, it is within that "impact zone" (below the 1.5 m threshold).
  3. The Result: Because it is exposed and below 1.5 m, it must be protected by a raceway (like EMT or PVC conduit) or other approved mechanical means.
